How to Deal with Landslides

Landslides can be dangerous, but proper preparation and awareness can save lives. Follow these guidelines:

Preparation Checklist

Do's During a Landslide

  • Move to higher ground immediately if you suspect a landslide.
  • Listen to local authorities and follow their instructions.
  • Stay alert for unusual sounds like cracking trees or rumbling earth.

Don'ts During a Landslide

  • Do not stay near steep slopes or unstable terrain.
  • Do not attempt to drive through flooded or debris-filled roads.
  • Do not return to the affected area until it is declared safe.
